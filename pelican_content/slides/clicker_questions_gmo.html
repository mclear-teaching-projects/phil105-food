Genetically Modified Foods

A genetically modified food/crop is one that

  1. is grown or bred with a specific purpose in mind
  2. is manipulated at the molecular level to have traits that farmers or consumers desire
  3. contains no foreign genetic material
  4. is more dangerous to consume than non-genetically modified food

The 'unnaturalness' objection (UE) is a kind of extrinsic objection to GM food

  1. True
  2. False

People react to "negative information" concerning food choices by

  1. typically ignoring it
  2. giving it great significance in deliberation
  3. giving it too little significance in deliberation
  4. misunderstanding it
