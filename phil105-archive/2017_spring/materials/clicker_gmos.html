Genetically Modified Foods

A genetically modified food is one that

  1. is grown or bred with a specific purpose in mind
  2. is cultivated from materials that are not native to the place in which it is grown/raised
  3. contains foreign genetic material
  4. is more dangerous to consume than non-genetically modified food
